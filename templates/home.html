We know why you're here...

Everything requires passwords now, this is a given. Making passwords to the standards of the website you're using is a pain. We're here to help. "I Need a Password" is a password manager that will help you create and store passwords for every website you use. It's easy to use and will help keep your passwords safe.

How does it work?
expand_more

Simple!

Once you create a new account (button on the left there), you simply click "Create New Password" and fill out what is required. Hit "Save", and you're done! The site does the remembering for you! So, no more password books or sticky notes. Your password is strong and safe.
